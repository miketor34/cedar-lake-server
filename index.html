Features

Everything you need
on the water

📍
Live GPS Tracking
Continuously monitors your position using your device's high-accuracy GPS and updates your zone status in real time.
🔔
Audio Warning
Repeating beep alert triggers the moment you leave the acceptable zone, so you know without looking at the screen.
🗺️
Live Map
OpenStreetMap integration shows your exact position on a real map as you move across the lake.
🏊
Multiple Locations
Switch between Big Cedar Lake, Lake Geneva, Lake Mendota, Elkhart Lake, Lake Winnebago, MSOE, and Columbia Reserve.
📏
406ft Shore Rule
Enforces the standard 406-foot minimum distance from any shoreline for acceptable surf zone classification.
📱
Works on Any Device
Runs in any browser on iPhone, Android, or desktop. No app download required — just open and go.

How It Works

Simple as
three steps

1
Select your location
Pick the lake or zone you're heading to from the dropdown menu.
2
Hit Start Tracking
The app activates your GPS and begins sending your position to the server every few seconds.
3
Stay in the green
The zone card turns green when you're in the acceptable surf zone. A beep alarm fires if you drift too close to shore.
